How to Play - RumbleRoll

Objective: Be the first player to reach a score of 100 to win the game.

Gameplay:

  1. Starting the Game:

    The game begins with Player 1 Each player takes turns to roll a single six-sided dice.

  2. Roll Dice:
    • Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.
    • If the dice shows a 1, your turn ends immediately, and your current score for that turn becomes zero. It's now the other player's turn.
    • If the dice shows any other number (2 through 6), that number is added to your current turn's score.
  3. Hold Score:
    • At any point during your turn, you can choose to "Hold Score" by clicking the button.
    • When you hold your score, the total score for your turn is added to your overall score.
    • IMPORTANT: You can only choose to "Hold Score" if your current score is greater than 0. If your current score is 0, you can't switch to the other player.
    • It's now the other player's turn.
  4. Winning the Game:
    • The game continues with players taking turns, rolling the dice, and holding scores.
    • The first player to reach or exceed a total score of 100 wins the game immediately.
  5. Switching Players:
    • Players take alternate turns - Player 1, then Player 2, and so on.
    • When a player rolls a 1 or chooses to hold their score, it automatically becomes the other player's turn.

Winning Tip: Be strategic with your decisions to roll or hold to maximize your score and reach 100 points before your opponent.
